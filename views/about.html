Who this?

This is Corn, the Uni. The hero of this tale is a shy and loving unicorn that lives in a dreamy land adequately named... Dreamy Land. He’s in a hurry for some reason, so he’ll have to spread his tiny hooves to jump over sticky poopies, as well as to catch shooting stars that will absolutely make him high as a kite.
